About the Anusaran Online Library

We are happy to present ourselves as one of the free online book library. All true Christian work originates from the heart of God. Although man is inevitably a part of its outworking, it is God himself who initiates and makes known His purposes to individuals. This is the only possible explanation for the beginning of Anusaran.

Our desire is to make Evangelical Literature available to the nation, so that people may come to faith and maturity in the Lord Jesus Christ. We are trying to reach the masses through our online library.

As you go through our website, we assure you our best services. We are here to help you to get all the Christian literature in different languages and versions, the purpose of sharing God's love with the people of the world.

What is Anusaran?

Anusaran is a literature mission, a non-profit Inter-Denominational Christian organization dedicated to bringing Evangelical Literature to the people of the Nation in variety and volume, publishing in many languages, establishing book centers and operating book mobiles. These are among various methods used to forward the programme.

Anusaran is primarily involved in starting and operating Christian bookstores, free online download, publishing enterprises and mobile book ministry in cities and towns where there is critical need.

Anusaran partners with the Churches and other Christian missions in order to meet the literature needs of new and maturing Christians. This task is accomplished by a dedicated team of committed staff.
